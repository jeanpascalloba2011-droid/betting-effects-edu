Effects of Betting and Gambling

1.Financial Effects

Chasing losses leads to debt,borrowing from friends/family,selling personal items,and loss of saving.This can cause long-term financial instability.

2.Mental Health Effects

Gambling is linked to anxiety,depression,stress,guilt,and in severe cases,suicidal thoughts.The cycle of winning and losing creates emotional instability.

3.Social &Academic Effects

It damages relationships with family and friends.For students,it hurts concentration,causes missed classes,and leads to poor grades or dropping our.
