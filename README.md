
# Betting Effects Edu

An educational website about the financial, mental health, and social effects of betting and gambling, with a focus on youth in Kenya.

**Live site:** https://jeanpascalloba2011-droid.github.io/betting-effects-edu/

### Pages
- **Home** - Overview and quick facts
- **Effects** - Financial, mental health, and social impacts  
- **Get Help** - Crisis resources: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, Gamblers Anonymous, Kenya contacts
- **Contact** - How to reach the site creator

### Purpose
This project aims to raise awareness about gambling harms and connect people with help resources.

*Educational resource only. Not professional medical or financial advice.*
